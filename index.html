Welcome
Lewis Designs specializes in the design, manufacture, and installation of custom cabinets for every room of your home. Our custom cabinet business began in 1989 as a division of our custom home building company. Now exclusively a custom cabinet shop, our experience in home building and renovation gives us a unique perspective and level of expertise regarding your project. Working with the best Central Ohio craftsmen, contractors, architects, and designers, we create unique living spaces.
